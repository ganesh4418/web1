What is Graphic Design

Graphic design is the practise of using words, pictures, and other visual elements to communicate ideas to an audience, often in order to achieve a certain result.
Graphic design is a field of study, a profession, and an applied art whose work involves presenting visual messages to target audiences in order to achieve predetermined goals.
